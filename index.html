Knight Hawks Motorcycle Club

What Are We About

Here at Knight Hawks Motorcycle Club we foster a family-like environment This group is for the ladies that want to get out and ride with other local women. We're ladies from Southeastern VA & Eastern NC who enjoy riding our motorcycles. We are a diverse group of woman( professionals, careers, raising families, loving life.) To be a part of this group, you should be female with a motorcycle or in the process of getting her 'M' class and a motorcycle to ride. All bikes and all experience levels are welcome. You'll find plenty of support from members if you're just getting started. Don't be afraid to ask us for help. We've all been there! It's all about enjoying your own ride while staying safe. We don't leave anyone behind. Join us today, and RSVP to a meetup right away. I promise, you'll find a group of amazing women, all in love with the open road. Welcome to Tidewater Ladies on Wheels. We ride our own!
