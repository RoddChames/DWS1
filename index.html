Who We Are

This group is for those who want to get out and ride with others who share the joy of cruising around the city on two wheels. We are a growing establishment from Southeastern Virginia and Eastern North Carolina and we are continuously looking for ways to impact our communities in a positive manner. To be a part of this fine establishment, you should have a motorcycle or be in the process of obtaining a motorcycle to ride. All types of motorcycle and all experience levels are welcomed to come out and at least experience what we are all about here at Knight Hawks MC. If you are just getting started with your riding experience do not worry, because you will find plenty of support and encouragement from our members. Do not be afraid to ask us for help. We have all been there! It is all about enjoying your own ride while staying safe. We will not leave anyone behind. Join us today!!! We promise, you will find a group of amazing people who all love the open road.

Importance of Safety Gear

To improve motorcycle safety many countries mandate the wearing of personal protective equipment such as protective clothing and helmets. Protective clothing may include certain types of jackets, gloves, boots, and pants. Jackets meant for motorcyclists are typically made of leather or specialized man-made fabrics like cordura or Kevlar. These jackets typically include heavy padding on the elbow, spine, and shoulder regions. Gloves are generally made of leather or Kevlar and some include carbon fiber knuckle protection. Boots, especially those for sport riding, include reinforcement and plastic caps on the ankle and toe areas. Pants are usually leather, cordura, or Kevlar. Except for helmets, none of these items are required by law in any state in the USA, or in any part of the UK but are recommended by many of those who ride.

Membership

Riders must have the following:

  1. A valid MSF Card.
  2. Valid Drivers License with "M" Endorsement.
  3. A Road Safe Motorcycle.
